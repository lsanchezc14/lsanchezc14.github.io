About

My current job focus on software development with Java technologies, part of a collaboration team that uses Scrum as framework to handle priorities and deliver value fast. Also, as part of my university master's program, I am working with Machine Learning projects such as recurrent neural networks for time series problems. Academia has allowed me to improve my data analysis skills and tool expertise, such as with Python and SQL. In my first professional years, I worked on projects for the Gas and Oil industry, where I gained training and experience in data acquisition from field instrumentation on hazardous environments, monitoring and control systems. I worked extensively with SCADA/DCS, SQL and OPC communication, in conjunction with different applications and programming languages, such as C, Visual Basic, Python, Java and LabVIEW. Over the past 9 years, I have gained experience in different professional skills, such as sales, engineering projects, maintenance projects, customer service and leadership. Due to my experience, I have been part of mentorship programs and provided training to engineers and customers in Peru, Colombia, Chile, Mexico and the United States.
